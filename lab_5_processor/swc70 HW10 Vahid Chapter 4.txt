4.15) Compute the memory needed in bytes to store a 4-bit digital encoding of a 3-second analog audio signal sampled every 10 milliseconds:
* Samples over 3s. 10ms is 0.01s. So 3s/(0.01s/sample) = 300 samples
* 300 samples * 4bits/sample = 1200 bits
* 1200 bits is 150 bytes (divide by 8)
* The memory needed to store the encoded audio signal is 150 bytes
